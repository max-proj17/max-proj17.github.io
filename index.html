About Me

Hello, I'm Maximelio Finch, an Electrical & Computer Engineering major at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2024.

Specializing in embedded systems design and adept in a variety of programming languages, including Java, C, Python, and C++, my academic and project work has consistently focused on the intersection of hardware functionality and software innovation.

An Eagle Scout and a disciplined martial artist with a black belt in Shotokan Karate, my approach to engineering is grounded in precision and excellence. My professional experiences, such as being an undergraduate computer engineer for the TRACERS NASA Program and developing applications with Holo Reality Labs, reflect a commitment to leveraging emerging technologies to solve complex problems.

I am skilled in utilizing tools like OpenCV and TensorFlow, and I apply machine learning models to improve processes and products. As a member of the IEEE Robotics & Automation Society and a leader in the Robotics Club, I collaborate on and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in robotics and automation.
