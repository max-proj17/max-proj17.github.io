About Me

Hello, I'm Maximelio Finch, an Electrical & Computer Engineering major at the University of Iowa, with an anticipated graduation date in May 2024. My passion lies in embedded systems design, particularly with an eye towards the integration of AI in robotic systems.

Proficient in programming languages such as Java, C, Python, and C++, my academic journey and project engagements have been steered towards the intersection of hardware efficiency and software innovation.

While I'm adept in using tools such as OpenCV and PyTorch for applying machine learning models, I am currently working on projects that delve into my areas of interest: non-linear control, reinforcement learning, path-planning, and enhancing perception in robotic systems. As a member of the IEEE Robotics & Automation Society and computer vision lead in the Robotics Club, I am dedicated to contributing to projects that push the boundaries of robotics and automation, aiming to integrate embedded systems with advanced AI technologies for groundbreaking solutions.
